A snapshot of adoption, wins, and the path to scaling across product, design, and research.

What we want to measure

01
Are we improving consistency?
Is there more consistency between what is designed and what makes it to production?
  • How often the shipped UI differs meaningfully from the approved design
  • Does the handover include everything dev needs, rated by the receiving team
  • How many design changes are requested within 2 weeks of launch
02
Are we moving faster?
Is this AI driven process actually helping teams move quicker?
  • Time from kickoff to first prototype
  • Time from kickoff to first ship
  • Prototype-to-handover time
03
Do teams feel confident in the process?
We want to hear directly from the teams on what's working and what could be better.
  • How confident do teams feel using the prototypes in their workflow
  • How has this changed the way Designers and Engineers work together
  • Does everyone involved have what they need
04
Can this scale beyond a few teams?
We're starting with a small group, but the goal is for this to work across the org without needing hand-holding at every step.
  • How quickly can a new team get up and running independently
  • Are teams reusing shared patterns and components or starting from scratch each time
  • How much support does each new team need to ship their first prototype
